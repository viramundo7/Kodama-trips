About Na güará

Less planning. More climbing.

Climbing trips have become complicated — too much planning, too many messages, too much friction before you even get outside.

Na güará was created to remove that friction.

We organize climbing weekends for experienced climbers by handling the logistics around the trip.

You join a structured group, follow a clear plan, and focus entirely on climbing.

No guiding. No coaching. Just better-organized climbing weekends with the right people.

Trip Policy

Weather

If conditions are unsafe for climbing, the trip will be rescheduled. Your booking will be transferred to the new date.

Cancellations

If you cancel your spot, we will try to fill it with another participant.

If your place is filled, you will receive a full refund. If it cannot be filled, a partial refund may apply depending on timing.
