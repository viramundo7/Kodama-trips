Hi, I'm Pedro.

I'm a climber based in Berlin, and I created Na güará because climbing trips have become more complicated than the climbing itself.

Too much planning, too many messages, finding partners, coordinating transport, choosing a destination, and organizing logistics before anyone even touches the rock.

Na güará was created to remove that friction.

I organize climbing weekends for independent climbers by bringing people together and handling the planning around the trip — from destination selection and crag information to accommodation suggestions and travel guidance.

The goal is simple: join a structured group, follow a clear plan, and focus on climbing.

No guiding. No coaching. Just better-organized climbing weekends with the right people.

Trip Policy

Weather

If conditions are unsafe for climbing, the trip will be rescheduled.

Cancellations

If your place is filled, you will receive a full refund. If it cannot be filled, a partial refund may apply depending on timing.
