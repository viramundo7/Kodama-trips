About Na güará

Less planning. More climbing.

Climbing trips often take more effort to organize than the climbing itself. Finding partners, planning logistics, and coordinating everything can get in the way of actually getting outside.

Na güará was created to remove that friction.


We handle:

• Curated climbing destinations
• Crag access and planning
• Accommodation suggestions
• Travel guidance and coordination
• Group organization for experienced climbers


The goal is simple: make it easier to spend more time climbing, and less time planning.

How it works

We design each trip around a specific climbing destination.

Once a trip is announced, you receive all the information needed to join — including travel suggestions and accommodation options.

You book your own transport and stay. We keep it flexible so you can choose what works best for you.

We take care of the climbing side — selecting crags, checking access, and organizing the structure of the weekend.

You show up ready to climb.

What this is

Na güará is a self-organized climbing trip framework for experienced climbers.

You are responsible for your own climbing, safety, and equipment.


This is not:

• A guided climbing course
• A coaching service
• A beginner experience
• A supervised activity

What to bring

Keep it simple — focus on climbing.

Climbing Equipment

• Harness

• Climbing shoes

• Helmet

• Belay device + carabiner

• Chalk + chalk bag

• Rope (60 meters, higher routes 70 meters recommended)

• Personal anchor

• Quickdraws (6 minimum)

• Locking carabiners (3 recommended)

Food & Hydration

Food, snacks, and water are your responsibility for the trip. Make sure you bring enough to stay fueled and hydrated throughout the climbing days

• Water bottle

• Electrolytes (recommended)

• Light meals for travel days

Travel Essentials

• ID / passport

• Travel tickets

• Personal insurance (recommended)

• Layared clothing (Windbreaker/ waterproof shell, approach shoes,light insulated layer)

Sunscreen

Overnight

• Tent

• Sleeping bag

• Sleeping pad

• Cooking equipment
