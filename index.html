About Na güará

Less planing. More climbing.

Climbing trips have become complicated, and we felt there had to be a better way — so we created Na güará. We handle logistics, travel, accommodation, and access, so you don’t have to. The goal is simple: make it easier to spend more time climbing and less time planning.

How it works

Simple, flexible, and focused on climbing.

We design each trip around a destination and a climbing experience. Once a trip is announced, you’ll receive all the details you need to join — including travel options and accommodation suggestions.


You book your own transport and stay using our recommendations. This keeps things flexible so you can choose what works best for you.


We take care of the climbing side — selecting the crags, checking access, and planning the days on the rock so everything is ready when you arrive.


You show up ready to climb. Everything else is already handled.

What to bring

Keep it simple — focus on climbing.

Climbing Equipment

• Harness

• Climbing shoes

• Helmet

• Belay device + carabiner

• Chalk + chalk bag

• Rope (60 metters recommended)

• Personal anchor

• Quickdraws (12 per route, 6 per person)

• Locking carabiners (3 recommended)

Food & Hydration

Food, snacks, and water are your responsibility for the trip. Make sure you bring enough to stay fueled and hydrated throughout the climbing days

• Water bottle

• Electrolytes (recommended)

• Light meals for travel days

Travel Essentials

• ID / passport

• Travel tickets

• Personal insurance (recommended)

• Layared clothing (Windbreaker/ waterproof shell, approach shoes,light insulated layer.

Sunscreen
