Hi, I’m Pedro.

I’m a climber based in Berlin. I started Na güará because climbing trips often took more time than climbing itself.

Finding partners, organizing travel, checking access, and coordinating weekends killed momentum.

Na güará is my way of fixing that.

Not a guide service. Not a course.

Just structured weekends for people who already climb.

⚠️ Independent Climbers Only — No guiding, no coaching. You must be able to climb outdoors safely and independently.
